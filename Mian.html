About

Dear user, I've been playing Rec Room for about 5 years now. During this time, I've encountered various issues such as bans, timeouts, voice bans, etc., for seemingly no reason.

It's important to note that this website is not intended to be a dox of Rec Room. I have immense respect for Rec Room, understanding that certain aspects on this website are beyond their control.

The purpose of this website is to aid Rec Room's improvement as a platform. It serves as a means to inform them about occurrences in the game that they might not be aware of. In other words, just constructive criticism.

Thank you.
